Our Story

30 Years of Homestyle Hospitality

Maryanne opened these doors in 1993 with a simple promise: serve real, made-from-scratch food in a place that feels like home. Three decades later, that promise hasn't changed.

Using quality ingredients sourced locally when possible, every dish is crafted with care. From our signature buttermilk pancakes to Sunday supper, you'll taste the difference that real cooking makes.
