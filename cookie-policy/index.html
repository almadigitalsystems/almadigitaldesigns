Cookie Policy

Effective Date: April 3, 2026  ·  Last Updated: April 4, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and understand how you use the site.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use the following categories of cookies on almadigitaldesigns.com:

Type Purpose Examples Duration
Strictly Necessary Core site functionality — these cannot be disabled Session state, security tokens Session
Analytics Understand how visitors use the site (Google Analytics 4, Measurement ID: G-CF23T8HPJG) Page views, time on site, traffic source Up to 2 years
Marketing / Outreach Track email engagement via Instantly.ai Email open/click tracking Per provider settings

Non-essential cookies (Analytics, Marketing) are only loaded after you give consent via our cookie banner.

3. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time:

  • Cookie banner: When you first visit, you can accept or decline non-essential cookies.
  • Cookie Settings: Use the Cookie Settings link in our site footer to update your preferences at any time.
  • Browser settings: You can also control cookies through your browser. Note that disabling all cookies may affect site functionality.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We use a limited number of trusted third-party services that may set their own cookies:

  • Google Analytics 4 — analytics. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Instantly.ai — email outreach tracking. See Instantly.ai DPA.
