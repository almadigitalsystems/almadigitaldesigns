1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function, remember your preferences, and provide analytics data to help us improve the site.

We also use local storage (a similar browser technology) to remember your cookie consent choice.

3. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

When you first visit our website, a cookie consent banner will appear. You can choose to:

  • Accept All — enable all cookies including analytics and marketing
  • Reject Non-Essential — only essential cookies are used
  • Manage Preferences — choose which categories to enable individually

You can update your preferences at any time by clicking Cookie Settings in the website footer or at the top of this page.

You may also control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.
